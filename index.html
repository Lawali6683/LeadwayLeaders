Our Policy & Human Rights Charter

At Leadway Peace Functions and Human Rights Aids, our mission is to promote and defend human rights, ensuring dignity and peace for all. This policy outlines our commitment to these values.

1. Non-Discrimination Policy

We are dedicated to providing support without discrimination based on race, religion, gender, ethnicity, or political affiliation. Our aid is for all who need it, guided by principles of neutrality and impartiality.

2. Transparency and Accountability

We believe in open governance. All our activities, from financial operations to aid distribution, are conducted with full transparency. We are accountable to the communities we serve and our partners.

3. Protection of Vulnerable Groups

We prioritize the safety and rights of children, women, and other vulnerable groups. We ensure our programs are designed to protect them from harm and empower them to thrive.

4. Data Privacy

The privacy of our beneficiaries and supporters is paramount. We collect and use data with strict confidentiality, ensuring it is protected and never shared without consent.

5. Peaceful Conflict Resolution

Our core approach is to foster peace through dialogue and education. We actively work to resolve conflicts non-violently and promote mutual understanding between different groups.
